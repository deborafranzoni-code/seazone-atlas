# Como posso indicar um terreno

[Como fazer a indição de um terreno.pdf](Como%20posso%20indicar%20um%20terreno%20d4582bcb3555456b8e42f25f56026093/Como_fazer_a_indio_de_um_terreno.pdf)

Este guia fornece instruções passo a passo sobre como fazer uma indicação de terrenos por meio do Sapron. Seguindo estes passos e fornecendo todas as informações necessárias, a sua recomendação de terreno será recebida e processada pelos times responsáveis.


1. Acesse o site <https://sapron.com.br/login>
2. Insira seu login e senha e clique em "Entrar".

3. Para realizar a indicação de terrenos, você pode ir através de dois caminhos: o primeiro é pelo caminho abaixo, onde no canto inferior da página inicial, você deverá clicar em "Indicar".

 ![https://ajeuwbhvhr.cloudimg.io/colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2023-12-13/2a4c85bc-3216-44c1-8aed-755c99573c1e/user_cropped_screenshot.jpeg?tl_px=297,171&br_px=1588,893&force_format=png&width=1120.0&wat=1&wat_opacity=0.7&wat_gravity=northwest&wat_url=https://colony-recorder.s3.us-west-1.amazonaws.com/images/watermarks/FB923C_standard.png&wat_pad=524,529](https://ajeuwbhvhr.cloudimg.io/colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2023-12-13/2a4c85bc-3216-44c1-8aed-755c99573c1e/user_cropped_screenshot.jpeg?tl_px=297,171&br_px=1588,893&force_format=png&width=1120.0&wat=1&wat_opacity=0.7&wat_gravity=northwest&wat_url=https://colony-recorder.s3.us-west-1.amazonaws.com/images/watermarks/FB923C_standard.png&wat_pad=524,529)


4. Selecione o tipo de indicação que deseja, nesse caso, para realizaar indicações de terrenos será: "Indicar Terreno"."

 ![https://ajeuwbhvhr.cloudimg.io/colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2023-12-13/8757af07-aa5f-4943-a03a-8fa72aaf6415/ascreenshot.jpeg?tl_px=251,50&br_px=1542,771&force_format=png&width=1120.0&wat=1&wat_opacity=0.7&wat_gravity=northwest&wat_url=https://colony-recorder.s3.us-west-1.amazonaws.com/images/watermarks/FB923C_standard.png&wat_pad=524,277](https://ajeuwbhvhr.cloudimg.io/colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2023-12-13/8757af07-aa5f-4943-a03a-8fa72aaf6415/ascreenshot.jpeg?tl_px=251,50&br_px=1542,771&force_format=png&width=1120.0&wat=1&wat_opacity=0.7&wat_gravity=northwest&wat_url=https://colony-recorder.s3.us-west-1.amazonaws.com/images/watermarks/FB923C_standard.png&wat_pad=524,277)


5. O segundo caminho é na página inicial, selecione a categoria: "Indicar Terreno".

 ![https://ajeuwbhvhr.cloudimg.io/colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2023-12-13/d99a2d30-8256-4680-ab6d-4c172a71859a/user_cropped_screenshot.jpeg?tl_px=0,135&br_px=1290,856&force_format=png&width=1120.0&wat=1&wat_opacity=0.7&wat_gravity=northwest&wat_url=https://colony-recorder.s3.us-west-1.amazonaws.com/images/watermarks/FB923C_standard.png&wat_pad=107,277](https://ajeuwbhvhr.cloudimg.io/colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2023-12-13/d99a2d30-8256-4680-ab6d-4c172a71859a/user_cropped_screenshot.jpeg?tl_px=0,135&br_px=1290,856&force_format=png&width=1120.0&wat=1&wat_opacity=0.7&wat_gravity=northwest&wat_url=https://colony-recorder.s3.us-west-1.amazonaws.com/images/watermarks/FB923C_standard.png&wat_pad=107,277)


6. Ao ser redirecionado para o formulário de indicação, preencha todos os dados (que você tiver) do terreno a ser indicado.

 ![https://ajeuwbhvhr.cloudimg.io/colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2023-12-13/f7a089c9-9c92-4808-b951-9cb87159564a/ascreenshot.jpeg?tl_px=84,68&br_px=1374,789&force_format=png&width=1120.0&wat=1&wat_opacity=0.7&wat_gravity=northwest&wat_url=https://colony-recorder.s3.us-west-1.amazonaws.com/images/watermarks/FB923C_standard.png&wat_pad=524,277](https://ajeuwbhvhr.cloudimg.io/colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2023-12-13/f7a089c9-9c92-4808-b951-9cb87159564a/ascreenshot.jpeg?tl_px=84,68&br_px=1374,789&force_format=png&width=1120.0&wat=1&wat_opacity=0.7&wat_gravity=northwest&wat_url=https://colony-recorder.s3.us-west-1.amazonaws.com/images/watermarks/FB923C_standard.png&wat_pad=524,277)


7. Ao final, clique em "Enviar".

 ![https://ajeuwbhvhr.cloudimg.io/colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2023-12-13/2df6de4a-cf8d-4704-bcbd-65aafdb9009d/ascreenshot.jpeg?tl_px=445,171&br_px=1735,893&force_format=png&width=1120.0&wat=1&wat_opacity=0.7&wat_gravity=northwest&wat_url=https://colony-recorder.s3.us-west-1.amazonaws.com/images/watermarks/FB923C_standard.png&wat_pad=524,443](https://ajeuwbhvhr.cloudimg.io/colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2023-12-13/2df6de4a-cf8d-4704-bcbd-65aafdb9009d/ascreenshot.jpeg?tl_px=445,171&br_px=1735,893&force_format=png&width=1120.0&wat=1&wat_opacity=0.7&wat_gravity=northwest&wat_url=https://colony-recorder.s3.us-west-1.amazonaws.com/images/watermarks/FB923C_standard.png&wat_pad=524,443)
